Amos  1 : 1 - 15

AMOS

1 The words of A′mos,* who was among the sheep raisers from Te·ko′a, which he received in vision concerning Israel in the days of King Uz·zi′ah of Judah and in the days of Jer·o·bo′am son of Jo′ash, the king of Israel, two years before the earthquake. 2 He said:

“Jehovah will roar out of Zion,

And he will raise his voice out of Jerusalem.

The pastures of the shepherds will mourn,

And the summit of Car′mel will dry up.”

 3 “This is what Jehovah says,

‘“For three revolts* of Damascus, and for four, I will not reverse it,

Because they threshed Gil′e·ad with iron threshing sledges.

 4 So I will send a fire upon the house of Haz′a·el,

And it will consume the fortified towers of Ben-ha′dad.

 5 I will break the bars of Damascus;

I will destroy the inhabitants from Bik′ath-a′ven

And the one ruling* from Beth-e′den;

And the people of Syria will go as exiles to Kir,” says Jehovah.’

 6 This is what Jehovah says,

‘“For three revolts of Gaz′a, and for four, I will not reverse it,

Because they took a whole group of exiles to hand them over to E′dom.

 7 So I will send a fire onto the wall of Gaz′a,

And it will consume her fortified towers.

 8 I will destroy the inhabitants from Ash′dod,

And the one ruling* from Ash′ke·lon;

I will turn my hand against Ek′ron,

And the remaining Phi·lis′tines will perish,” says the Sovereign Lord Jehovah.’

 9 This is what Jehovah says,

‘For three revolts of Tyre, and for four, I will not reverse it,

Because they handed over a whole group of exiles to E′dom,

And because they did not remember the covenant of brothers.

10 So I will send a fire onto the wall of Tyre,

And it will consume her fortified towers.’

11 This is what Jehovah says,

‘For three revolts of E′dom, and for four, I will not reverse it,

Because he pursued his own brother with the sword,

And because he refused to show mercy;

In his anger he keeps tearing them apart relentlessly,

And he remains furious with them continually.

12 So I will send a fire into Te′man,

And it will consume the fortified towers of Boz′rah.’

13 This is what Jehovah says,

‘“For three revolts of the Am′mon·ites, and for four, I will not reverse it,

Because they ripped open the pregnant women of Gil′e·ad to widen out their own territory.

14 So I will set fire to the wall of Rab′bah,

And it will consume her fortified towers,

With a war cry in the day of battle,

With a tempest in the day of storm wind.

15 And their king will go into exile together with his princes,” says Jehovah.’

^ Amos 1:1 Meaning “Being a Load” or “Carrying a Load.”

^ Amos 1:3 Or “crimes.”

^ Amos 1:5 Lit., “the one holding the scepter.”

^ Amos 1:8 Lit., “the one holding the scepter.”
